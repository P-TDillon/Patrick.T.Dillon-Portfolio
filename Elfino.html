This Unity based project was used as part of a marketing campaign by a major beauty company with a comedy theme where an imaginary firm of lawyers were acting on behalf of all the customers that had been ripped off by rival beauty companies who charged too much for substandard beauty products. The "ticker" is a rolling total of all the money that people said they wasted. I worked on the code for the constant cylinder rotation with accelerating motion when a claim is filed via a linked iPad. It was displayed at the Oculus World Trade Center in New York City.
